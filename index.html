Snakes and Ladders

HOW TO PLAY


Try to win the race and get to the end before Jazzy Crock!

To start the game and find out who goes first, click the START GAME button.

If you are going first, throw the dice. Be polite, and wait for Jazzy Crock to move before throwing the dice again.

Try to find a LADDER, it will help you reach the end quicker.

But be careful! If you come accross a SNAKE, you will need to run away.

You can turn the pop up with information about Croc's move off by clicking Turn Info toggle button at any time during the game.


Choose Your avatar, Blue or Sunny:

Note: If you want to change your avatar later, click on the help button and select your avatar again.


Good luck!
