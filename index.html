Snakes and Ladders

HOW TO PLAY
You will be playing against the computer. To start, first choose your avatar. When you made your choice, "throw" the dice (click it) to play for who goes first. Then wait for you turn and click the dice to throw it again. Your avatar will move by the number shown on the dice. If you stand on the field showing a ladder you will move forward to the indicated filed. If you come accross, it will bite you and make you move down. To win, you need to reach the END first. Good luck!
