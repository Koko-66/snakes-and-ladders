Snakes and Ladders

HOW TO PLAY


Try to win the race and get to the end before Jazzy Crock!

To start the game and find out who goes first, click the START GAME button.

If you are starting, throw the dice. Wait for Jazzy Crock to move before throwing the dice again for next round.

Try to find a LADDER! you will then move up to the field above.

But be careful! If you come accross a SNAKE, it will bite you and you will need to run away and go back to the field below.


Now you choose Your avatar, Blue or Sunny:

Note: If you want to change your avatar later, click on the help button and select your avatar again.


Good luck!
