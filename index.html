Snakes and Ladders

HOW TO PLAY


Try to win the race and get to the end before Jazzy Crock!

To start the game and find out who goes first, click the START GAME button.

If you are going first, throw the dice. Wait for Jazzy Crock to move before throwing the dice again.

Try to find a LADDER, you will move one field up and can reach the end quicker.

But be careful! If you come accross a SNAKE, you will need to run away and will move one field down.


Choose Your avatar, Blue or Sunny:

Note: If you want to change your avatar later, click the avatar on the board or the help button and select it again.


Good luck!
